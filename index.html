Grass. multiplies over free matrixes, changes color depending on weather conditions
Grasseater. Can move on free matrixes, has its own Energy that grows by eating Grass and decreases during movement
Dog. Can move on free matrixes and grasses, has its own Energy, which grows at the expense of destroying Predator and decreases during movement. Protects Grasseater
Predator. Can move on free matrixes, has its own Energy that grows by eating Grass and Grasseater and decreases during movement
Bomb. Placed in one of the random cells of the matrix, and when the Grasseater stands on the Bombs the game refreshes, and it all starts again
Deminer. Can move on free matrix and grasses, When an Bomb is detected, it destroys it, protecting the Grasseater
